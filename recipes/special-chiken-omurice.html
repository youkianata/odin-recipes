special chiken Omurice box


Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g cooked rice
  • 50 g cooked chicken or ham (optional)
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Additional ketchup for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the filling: Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the onion and bell pepper until softened. Add the cooked chicken or ham if using, and cook for another minute. Stir in the cooked rice and ketchup, mixing well. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Make the omelette: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt. Heat a non-stick pan with a little oil over medium heat. Pour in the beaten eggs, swirling to create an even layer. Cook until the edges start to set but the center is still slightly runny.
  3. Assemble the omurice: Spoon the rice filling onto one half of the omelette. Carefully fold the other half over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Gently slide the omurice onto a plate, seam side down.
  4. Garnish with additional ketchup in a cute pattern or simply drizzle it on top. Serve immediately while warm!

Tip: You can customize the filling with your favorite ingredients like mushrooms, peas, or cheese for extra flavor.
